What Is Site Preparation in Building Construction?

The first thing we need to get out of the way is what construction site preparation is.

It is essentially the process used to prepare a plot of land for commercial development and involves a number of crucial steps, including grading, filling, and leveling the soil to produce a sturdy and even surface. Land clearing is the process of removing trees, plants, rocks, and debris from the ground of the construction site.

As part of this process, drainage systems are installed to control water flow and stop soil erosion during heavy rainfall. This ensures that the land meets the requirements for supporting the new foundation and intended new building construction.
